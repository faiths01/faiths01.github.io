The Objective

Design, finish, and print a custom bookmark to be delivered to Marticville Middle School.


My Work

We were given a mission to create a bookmark for students at a local middle school. To the right is my design.

I used the resources I was given to help create my design. We were given a bunch of clipart designs by our professor we were allowed domain over. The owl was one of the resources I was given to work with. I saw it as a perfect centeral piece for my design.

Working within the theme, I used a decorative font with the word "who?". I then changed the opacity to blend it into the background.

Lastly, they needed something something inspiring, so I added the quote you see. "The greatest learners, Ask the most question".

Printing Process

The most commonly used method for commercial printing is Offset lithography. This is created with an oil-based ink as well as water so the ink wont adhere to the non-image areas. This method allows for quick production.

Ink jet printing is your sandard printer. We could have used this method instead but offset lithography has a quicker output of finished papers. The only downside is the setup time is a bit more tedious.
