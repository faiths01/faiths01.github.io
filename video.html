My Project

We were assigned a group to film, edit, and create our promotional videos. We created ours on the Millersville Pond.

This is one of the most iconic parts of our campus. The home of our Swans, the unofficial mascot, and many ducks as well. The Swans have been apart of this campus since 1966. The first swans were names Fred and Ethel. Once they passed away, Miller and S'ville became the new names for all of the swans to come.

This pond is a center piece, not only for millersville students, but many locals around the area who come to visit. Some go to the pond to study, others to feed the ducks, and all to enjoy the beauty of our campus.
